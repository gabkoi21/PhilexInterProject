Things You Should Know About Philex

Your trusted partner in comprehensive business solutions since 2017

Phelix International Multipurpose Company was founded in 2017 and officially obtained all legal documentation in 2023, marking the grand opening of its office. Established by Mr. Philip Topor, the company aims to create job opportunities and provide essential services on a global scale. It supports government initiatives in alignment with Liberian law through legal frameworks. The company has been accredited by key legal institutions, including the Liberia Revenue Authority, Justice Ministry, Liberia National Police, and the Liberia Business Registry.

Phelix International Multipurpose Company specializes in various sectors, offering a wide range of services. Operating both locally and internationally, Phelix International Multipurpose Company upholds high standards in compliance with regulatory laws. The company is built on the core values of transparency, accountability, and trust, ensuring strong relationships with clients and partners.
