Our Services

Real Estate Management

We specialize in real estate management, including hotels and guesthouses. Our services cover managing empty land, property buying and selling, and renting furnished office spaces and core office spaces.

Construction Engineering

We construct buildings of all kinds, including roads, drainages, mini bridges, and general renovations.

Professional Land Surveys

Our services include professional land surveys, land layouts, prepared map deeds, GPS surveys with coordinates, Google Earth mapping, total station surveys, and cadastral surveys.

Security Guard and Consultancy

We provide VIP protection and security services for companies, private homes, businesses, and transportation of goods and services.

Shipping and Travel Agency

We handle clearance controls, mediate and engage in airline ticketing and delivery.

Social Counseling and Motivational Support

Our counseling services include marital counseling, motivational counseling, and ecological counseling.

Sanitation and Maintenance Services

We maintain homes with house helpers (nurses) and provide road maintenance for public buildings, hospitals, private homes, and more.

Car Rental Services

We offer a variety of car rental services, including VIP and ordinary vehicles.

Old Folks Home and Healthcare Services

We manage elderly care facilities with both local and international healthcare services.

Micro Loan Services

We offer legal business support and financial assistance through micro-loans.

Agriculture

We manage farms, including row crops and farm production, as well as animal housing facilities.

Accounting and Economic Consultancy

We provide expert accounting and economic advisory services.

Vocational School

We offer vocational training and educational programs.

Logistics and Supply

Our services include managing logistics, transportation, and supply chain operations.

Monitoring and Evaluation of Projects

We conduct project monitoring and evaluation to ensure efficiency and success.
